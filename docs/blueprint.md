# **App Name**: ProduceTrack

## Core Features:

- Dashboard: Display key metrics such as total PUCs, total harvested (tons), average packout percentage, and total export value.
- Producer Portal: Provide a portal for producers to manage their information and access relevant tools.
- Producers: Enable management of producer data and related information.
- PUC Management: Manage Produce Unit Codes (PUCs) including creation, tracking, and updates.
- Inspections: Schedule, record, and manage inspections of produce at various stages.
- Harvests: Track harvest data, including quantity harvested over time and current harvest journeys.
- Packhouse: Monitor and manage packhouse operations and data.
- Reports: Generate and view reports on key metrics and performance indicators.
- Size Per Mass: Analyze produce size and mass data for quality control.
- Demand Analysis: Analyze demand to better predict the future demand.
- Settings: Configure application-level settings and preferences.
- Your Profile: View and manage user profile information.
- Logout: Securely log out of the application.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep green (#1E3F1E) to reflect nature and freshness.
- Background color: Very light green (#F5F5DC), near white, for a clean look.
- Accent color: Teal (#008080) to highlight interactive elements and important data points.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for readability and a modern yet approachable feel.
- Use flat, line-based icons that are consistent and easily recognizable. Keep icon colors aligned with the primary color.
- Maintain a clean and organized layout with a left sidebar navigation and clear separation of content sections.
- Use subtle transitions and animations to provide feedback on user interactions and improve overall user experience.
